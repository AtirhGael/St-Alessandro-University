About Our Institute

A Commitment to Excellence

St. Alessandro University Institute (SAU) is dedicated to providing high-quality education, fostering research and innovation, and promoting ethical and social responsibility. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in their careers and make positive contributions to society.

St Alessandro Clinic is a private medical institution founded in 2021. Created in memory of Professor Alessandro Faldini, a famous Italian Orthopedic surgeon and mentor of the proprietors of St Alessandro: Dr Mutsu Venantius and Dr Florence Nsuh with an overall objective of providing exceptional medical care, advancing medical knowledge, and fostering community wellbeing through innovative practice and compassionate services.
